Music is an art form, and cultural activity, whose medium is sound. General definitions of music include common elements such as pitch (which governs melody and harmony), rhythm (and its associated concepts tempo, meter, and articulation), dynamics (loudness and softness), and the sonic qualities of timbre and texture (which are sometimes termed the "color" of a musical sound). Different styles or types of music may emphasize, de-emphasize or omit some of these elements. ‍
If you have a hobby of playing music then take up your musical instrument and choose some music and play it. ‍

BASIC JUDGING CRITERIA:

  • Interpretation/Musicianship – style, phrasing, tempo, dynamics, emotional involvement, expression, continuity, mood; balance with accompaniment (if appropriate)
  • Technique – artistry, intonation, rhythm/rhythmic accuracy, articulation, precision, consistency, attacks and releases, bowing, tonguing, fingering, fluency, embouchure, pedalling
  • Choice of literature; mastery of technical difficulty
  • Improvisation if appropriate
  • Memorization
